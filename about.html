ABOUT SILVER OAK INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

About Us

Silveroak international was founded on September 19, 2022 by a seasoned and qualified educationist in her quest to bring up children with enviable character and
excellent academic performance. It is located in the serene but fast developing suburbs of Akute. Beginning with a handful of pupils at our Nursery and Primary
section, over the years, we have grown in leaps and bounds, churning out pupils and students with enviable character and academic excellence. The school is poised
to provide dynamic academic training environment, adequate teaching equipment, in addition to competent and committed staff who are ready to carefully train
and shape our future leaders to the peak of their intellectual and spiritual glory. Silveroak provides a dynamic training environment that merges cutting edge
teaching methodologies and competent staff who carefully train and shape tomorrow's leaders. Our school applies an individualized child centered approach to
reach every child's cognitive, psychomotor, and psychosocial needs in other to ensure that their potentials are awakened and they each shine brilliantly within their strengths.
